Robust Logistics, Simplified

We handled the complex engineering so you can focus on saving lives.

1

Request in Seconds

Patients simply set the hospital location. Our PostGIS engine instantly calculates a radius and pings the nearest active donors.

2

Accept & Navigate

Donors receive a push notification. Upon accepting, they get turn-by-turn navigation directly to the hospital entrance.

3

Verify & Donate

The secure Triple Handshake (3 QR Scans) verifies arrival, medical suitability, and final donation. No fakes.
